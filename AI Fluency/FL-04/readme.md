# Portfolio Cases

## Case 1: AI Workflow Audit (FL-01)

### The Problem

Most people use AI without a clear workflow. I wanted to understand which recurring tasks should stay human-led, which benefit from AI collaboration, and which can be safely delegated or automated.

### What I Did

I audited my professional and academic workflow and grouped each recurring task into four categories: Just Me, Collaborate with AI, Delegate to AI, and Fully Automate. I documented the reasoning behind every classification and configured Claude as an AI Internship Assistant to support my workflow. I also completed Anthropic Academy's AI Fluency and Claude 101 courses to build a stronger foundation before applying AI to my projects.

### What Came of It

The audit became a repeatable framework that guides how I use AI across future internship assignments. Instead of treating AI as a shortcut, I use it where it improves speed while keeping important technical decisions under my control.
